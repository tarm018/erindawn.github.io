Professional Skills

Erin Dawn

A qualified personal trainer with a focus on circus skills Erin is fully qualified to teach professional classes over a range of circus disciplines

  • Doubles Trapeze
  • Dance Trapeze
  • Lo-cast Flying Trapeze
  • Swinging Trapeze
  • Lyra
  • Aerial Silks
  • Trick Bike
  • Globe and Rolla Bolla
  • Trampolining
  • Chinese Pole
